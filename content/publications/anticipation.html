  • HiPAS Pole Failure Model

    The pole failure model in GridLAB-D is designed to examine multiple failure modes. However, given that ground-level structural failures are reported as the most frequent, the current implementation only supports these. Other failure modes may be provided in future versions of GridLAB-D.
